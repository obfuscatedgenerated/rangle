Rangle Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026

This document supplements the ollieg.codes Privacy Policy and applies specifically to your use of the Rangle application.

By using Rangle, you agree to both this document, as well as the base ollieg.codes Privacy Policy.

1. Data We Collect

  • For Cloud Sync: If you log in to sync your saves, we store your platform's User ID alongside your saved game states and settings.
  • For Leaderboards (Discord Activity Only): When you play Rangle as a Discord Activity, we collect your basic public profile details (Username, Display Name, and Avatar image). We also automatically collect the ID of the specific Discord Server (Guild) where you opened the game.
  • Leaderboard Access: To view the leaderboard of the server you are currently playing in, we must verify your membership by requesting access to your Discord server list. When you grant this permission, we also use this list to automatically populate your scores onto the leaderboards of your other servers.

2. How We Use This Data

  • Profile details are used to display your identity on leaderboards.
  • Server Memberships: We use your Discord Server IDs for two purposes: 1) To securely authorise your access to server leaderboards, ensuring only verified members can view a community's scores. 2) To associate your game scores with the other communities you belong to, allowing you to seamlessly participate in their leaderboards.

3. Data Deletion As a reminder, you can delete all your Cloud data at any time by visiting https://cloud.ollieg.codes/goodbye.
