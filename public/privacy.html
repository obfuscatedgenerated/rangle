Rangle Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 22, 2026

This document supplements the ollieg.codes Privacy Policy and applies specifically to your use of the Rangle application.

By using Rangle, you agree to both this document, as well as the base ollieg.codes Privacy Policy.

1. Data We Collect

  • For Cloud Sync: If you log in to sync your saves, we store your platform's User ID alongisde your saved game states and settings.
  • For Leaderboards (Discord Activity Only): When you play Rangle as a Discord Activity, we also collect your basic public profile details (Username, Display Name, and Avatar image) and the ID of the specific Discord Server (Guild) where you opened the game.

2. How We Use This Data

  • Profile details are stored to display your identity on leaderboards.
  • Discord Server IDs: By choosing to open Rangle within a specific Discord server, you consent to sharing your scores on that server's local leaderboard. We do not scan or collect information about other Discord servers you may be a member of.

3. Data Deletion As a reminder, you can delete all your Cloud data at any time by visiting https://cloud.ollieg.codes/goodbye.
